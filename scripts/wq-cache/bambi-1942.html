Bambi is a 1942 American animated drama film produced by Walt Disney Productions and released by RKO Radio Pictures. The film is loosely based on the 1923 novel Bambi, A Life in the Woods by Austrian author Felix Salten. The plot centers on a baby fawn named Bambi who grows up in the wild after his mother is shot by hunters.

Directed by David Hand. Written by Perce Pearce and Larry Morey.
