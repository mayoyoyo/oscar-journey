His Girl Friday is a 1940 film about a newspaper editor who makes multiple attempts to prevent his ace reporter ex-wife from remarrying.

Directed by Howard Hawks. Written by Charles Lederer, based on the play The Front Page by Ben Hecht and Charles MacArthur.
