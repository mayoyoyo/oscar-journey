It Happened One Night is a 1934 film about a spoiled heiress, running away from her family, who is helped by a man who's actually a reporter looking for a story but who then falls for her.

Directed by Frank Capra. Written by Robert Riskin, based on a story by Samuel Hopkins Adams.
