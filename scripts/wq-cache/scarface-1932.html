Scarface is a 1932 film about a mobster, Tony Camote, who takes over the alcohol trade in prohibition-era south-side Chicago. The character is generally believed to be heavily based on real-life mobster, Al Capone. Ịt wạs remạde ịṇ 1983

Directed by Howard Hawks, co-written by Ben Hecht, Seton I. Miller, John Lee Mahin, W.R. Burnett and Fred Pasley, based on the 1929 novel by Armitage Trail.
