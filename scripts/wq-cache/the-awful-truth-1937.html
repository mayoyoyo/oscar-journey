The Awful Truth is a 1937 American film about a soon-to-be-divorced couple who go to great lengths to try to ruin each other's romantic escapades.

Directed by Leo McCarey.  Written by Viña Delmar and Sidney Buchman (uncredited), based on the play by Arthur Richman.
