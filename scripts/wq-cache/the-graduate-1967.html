The Graduate is a 1967 film about Ben, a recent college graduate who is talented but aimless, who is seduced by Mrs. Robinson, the wife of his father's business partner. As he embarks on a tawdry affair with her, he becomes increasingly disillusioned with his parents world and their expectations for him, and he finds himself falling for Mrs. Robinson's daughter Elaine.

Directed by Mike Nichols. Written by Charles Webb, Calder Willingham and Buck Henry.
