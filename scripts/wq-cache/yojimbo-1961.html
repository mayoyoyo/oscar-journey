Yojimbo is a 1961 film about a crafty ronin who comes to a town divided by two criminal gangs and decides to play them against each other to free the town.

Directed by Akira Kurosawa. Written by Akira Kurosawa and Ryuzo Kikushima.
Sword or pistol? The fateful showdown!
